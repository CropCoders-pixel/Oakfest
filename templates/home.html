About Us

Welcome to our Farm-to-Table Marketplace, where we are committed to creating a more sustainable, eco-friendly future by connecting farmers directly with consumers. By promoting organic farming, reducing food waste, and fostering a circular economy, we‚Äôre building a platform that ensures fair pricing for farmers, better quality food for consumers, and a cleaner planet for all.



Our Vision

At the core of our marketplace is a vision to eliminate the middlemen in the food supply chain, giving farmers a direct connection to their consumers. This helps ensure fair prices for both farmers and consumers while promoting the consumption of organic, sustainably grown produce. We aim to reduce food waste through optimized inventory management and real-time availability of products. Our goal is to foster an eco-conscious community by promoting local, sustainable produce that not only nourishes individuals but also the planet.

What We Do

Organic Produce: We provide consumers with fresh, chemical-free produce, sourced directly from farms practicing sustainable farming methods. Our marketplace offers a wide range of organic food that is free from harmful chemicals like calcium carbide, commonly used in the ripening of fruits such as mangoes and bananas. We ensure that everything you buy is organic, healthy, and safe for consumption. Waste-to-Wealth: As part of our commitment to sustainability, we encourage consumers to deposit their biodegradable and non-biodegradable waste at our centers. Biodegradable waste is used to create compost and biogas, while non-biodegradable waste is repurposed into eco-friendly products like recycled plastic bottles. By doing so, we‚Äôre not only reducing waste but also turning it into a valuable resource that powers our marketplace. Consumers can also earn reward points for depositing waste, which can be used to purchase products on the app. Support for Small Businesses: We believe in promoting small-scale businesses that align with our sustainable and ethical values. Our platform features a variety of local artisans and eco-conscious entrepreneurs who produce handmade goods such as organic jaggery, eco-friendly clothes, and other sustainable products. This allows consumers to support small businesses while shopping for eco-friendly products.
